Early life

Gaius Julius Caesar was born on July 12, 100 BC. His name comes from the month of July. When Caesar was 16, his dad died making him the head of the family. One year later, he married Cornelia, the daughter of Marius. Caesar and Cornelia had one child, a daughter named Julia. Sulla, an enemy asked for Cornelia.Ceaser said no. Ceaser went to the army so he coudnt be captured. After Sulla died Ceaser left the army and went back to Rome.

Rise to power

In 59 BC, Julius entered politics. In 63 bc, Julius became a governor of Spain. Then he left and conquered Gaul after 9 years of fighting. Ceaser spent the next few years fighting his enemies in Africa, Spain and the Middle East. In 46 BC, Caesar became the dictator of Rome for the next 10 years. In 44 BC, he declared himself a dictator for life.
